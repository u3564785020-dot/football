Ticket FAQs

When do tickets go on sale?

Official FIFA ticket ballots open in phases, and verified marketplace inventory appears shortly afterward�join our list for priority alerts.

Are tickets mobile only?

Yes, all tickets are secure mobile passes delivered through the FIFA app or verified reseller wallet for instant access.

Can I buy hospitality packages?

Absolutely. VIP and hospitality bundles include premium seating, catering, and concierge travel�browse packages or contact our specialists.

Which stadium hosts the final?

MetLife Stadium in New York/New Jersey hosts the final on July 19, 2026 with 82,500 fans expected.

How much do World Cup tickets cost?

Pricing starts around $125 for group matches, $200 for early knockouts, $375 for semifinals, and $1,000+ for the final depending on seat location.

Can I resell my World Cup tickets?

FIFA typically allows official ticket resale through their platform closer to the tournament. Check the official FIFA ticketing portal for resale policies and authorized marketplaces.

What documents do I need to attend matches?

You'll need a valid passport, your mobile ticket, and potentially a Hayya Card (fan ID) which serves as your entry visa to host countries. Requirements will be confirmed closer to the tournament.

Are there ticket packages for multiple matches?

Yes, FIFA offers Follow My Team packages, Stadium Series bundles, and multi-match packages. Individual sellers also provide curated match combinations with travel options.

How early should I arrive at the stadium?

Stadium gates typically open 3 hours before kickoff. Arrive at least 90 minutes early for smooth entry, especially for high-demand matches like semifinals and the final.

Is parking available at World Cup venues?

Most stadiums offer limited parking with advance purchase required. Public transportation is strongly recommended, with many venues providing free shuttle services from designated areas.

Can children attend World Cup matches?

Yes, children are welcome but require their own ticket regardless of age. Some venues offer family sections with amenities suitable for young fans.

What if my match is postponed or cancelled?

FIFA's ticket terms include provisions for postponements and cancellations. Refunds or exchanges are typically offered according to official FIFA ticketing policies.

Are there accessible seating options?

All World Cup venues provide wheelchair-accessible seating and companion seats. These must be requested during ticket purchase and are subject to availability.

What payment methods are accepted?

Major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), debit cards, and digital payment methods are accepted. Some marketplaces also offer payment plans for higher-priced packages.

Can I transfer tickets to another person?

FIFA usually allows ticket transfers through their official platform, subject to certain conditions and deadlines. Name changes may require documentation.
